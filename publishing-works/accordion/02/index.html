Frequently
Asked
Questions

  • What do I do when I forget my password?

    You can use the questions you wrote when you first joined to find your password. If you don't remember your answer, we'll send you a link to set up a new password via a linked email.

  • Do I have to allow the use of cookies?

    You can choose as needed. However, if you allow cookies to be used, you will have a more comfortable connection. Cookie settings may vary depending on your browser, so check your preferences.

  • Where can I change my personal information?

    Please log in and go to My Page at the top right. You can change your personal information by entering the appropriate password one more time.

  • How do I solve the connection problem?

    Exit the window and try reconnecting. If it doesn't work, please leave an inquiry at the customer center. We'll deal with it quickly.

  • Where can I check the usage history?

    We are sorry, but for security reasons, we are unable to disclose personal customer records. It is prohibited according to company regulations. If you have any other inquiries, please leave them at the customer service center.
